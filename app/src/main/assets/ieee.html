About IEEE UNIVERSITY OF ALLAHABAD STUDENT CHAPTER:

The students of the University of Allahabad formed the IEEE Chapter on May 23rd, 2008. The bright students of the Centre of Computer Education, Institute of Professional Studies, University of Allahabad pledged not only to carry the Chapter forward but to make a mark at least amongst all the Indian IEEE Chapters. The Centre organises activities as per defined calendar regularly.

VISION:

To address all round development of the students by way of organising academic and career development activities on every Saturday and Sunday.

MISSION:

To place the Chapter and its Students at the top by organising academic competitions at the national and international level.
